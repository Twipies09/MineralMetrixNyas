F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the problem we are solving?

    The problem we are solving is to design an innovative approach to secure the supply of critical minerals in the transition to a fossil-free society..

  • What is our research plan?

    we will learn from past projects and delve into diverse mining methods such as deep sea mining. While making the logo is doable, our main concern is fakes and dupes. Our plan includes gathering data through surveys and experiments, applying statistical analysis, and partnering with experts. Peer review will also play a crucial role in the success of this project.

  • What is our concept?

    Our visionary concept involves establishing a government backed global online platform to promote sustainability in product manufacturing. Companies would undergo sustainability assessments, earning a distinctive logo akin to Fair Trade as a symbol of their commitment. Counterfeit checks would ensure logo authenticity.

  • What are the external forces that will impact your project?

    The governments can play a pivotal role in promoting the logo through media campaigns and incentives.
